Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Local Shop (“we”, “our”) operates the Local Shop mobile application. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account information (name, email, phone if provided)

How we use information

We use your data to provide the marketplace, process orders, support shop owners, and improve the service.

Account deletion

You can delete your account in the app under Profile → Delete Account.
